Our Story

A Taste of Colombia
in Boca Raton

Born in 2021 from a passion for Colombian culture and a love of bringing people together, El Patio is more than a restaurant — it's a gathering place. Founded by a proud Colombian family, every dish on our menu carries the flavors of home: the slow-simmered soups of Bogotá, the crispy chicharrón of Medellín, the warmth of a pan de bono fresh from the oven.

By day, El Patio is a relaxed family dining destination where you can start your morning with a Calentado and linger over Ajiaco at lunch. By night on weekends, the energy transforms — the full bar comes alive, DJ and karaoke fill the room, and our VIP lounge hosts private celebrations. Whether you're craving a $1.77 empanada or a full Bandeja Paisa, you'll always leave with a full stomach and a bigger smile.
